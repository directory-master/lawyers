Find and compare the best social security & disability attorneys in Georgia, ranked by rating and review volume. We list 29 social security & disability attorneys statewide. Social Security Disability (SSDI and SSI) is a federal program, so a Georgia disability lawyer can represent you from the first application through a hearing before an administrative law judge. Most work on contingency, and federal law caps their fee, so you pay only if you win benefits.
